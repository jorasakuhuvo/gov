Citizens' Rights

Citizens of the Republic of Jorasakuhuvo enjoy the following fundamental rights:

• Freedom of expression and peaceful political activity
• The right to vote and run for public office
• Equality before the law
• Protection of personal property
• The right to privacy
• The right to appeal government decisions
• Freedom to form associations (political parties are not permitted)
• The right to voluntarily renounce citizenship
• Exemption from compulsory military service
• Exemption from taxation
