History of the brave Vasians and their Jorasakuhuvo

The history of Jorasakuhuvo is closely connected with the emergence of the Vasian people in the early 21st century.

The following dates are recorded in the Gregorian calendar rather than Jorasakuhuvo's Tunovasok calendar.

Before Everything Started

Before the formation of the Vasian identity, the people who later became known as Vasians were members of the Han Chinese community. They had lived in mainland China for a long period of time and shared similar cultural and social experiences with the surrounding society. At that time, they generally regarded China as their homeland and expected their lives to develop in a similar way to those of many others.

Contact with the Wider World

During the 2020s, opportunities to study abroad and interact with the international community allowed some members of this community to encounter a broader range of global perspectives and information sources. These experiences encouraged deeper discussions about society, governance, and the future of their community.

Through these exchanges and reflections, a shared vision gradually emerged. On the night of April 13, 2025, members of the community reached a collective understanding to work toward building a society guided by fairness, equality, democracy, peace, and freedom. From that point forward, the concept of a new community identity began to take shape, and the idea of a nation known as the "Vasians" was formed.

The Road to Establishment

Establishing a new nation from the beginning required careful planning and gradual development. In the early stage, one member of the community offered their home as the first territory associated with this new national project. Over time, important foundations of the state were gradually developed, including a language, a currency, and a set of shared values.

On June 7, 2025, the arrangement and recording of "Potonyhuni Pe Vasok" (The Vasians' Song) were completed. The lyrics were written later and went through several revisions.

As the community continued to organize its institutions, an important milestone was reached on January 29, 2026. On that day, Pemife Noraane, elected as president, announced on the official website that the Provisional Government of Jorasakuhuvo had been formally established.

Towards Today

On February 2, 2026, three days after the founding announcement, President Pemife Noraane declared that Jorasakuhuvo's airspace would be reserved for military use.

On February 6, Jorasakuhuvian was established as the official language of the country and began to be used alongside English, which serves as an international language of communication.

On February 11, discussions arose regarding activities taking place near or within the territory of Jorasakuhuvo. Information related to the situation was released publicly, and the matter attracted attention from observers.

On February 12, representatives from Thailand held talks with Jorasakuhuvo. During the conversation, a representative commented that "Jorasakuhuvo is a very interesting country… it is a positive development for the Vasians."

On February 14, representatives associated with China entered the territory again without issuing a public statement and did not provide further comment at that time.

On February 17, a representative from Taiwan stated during a conversation with Jorasakuhuvo that "Jorasakuhuvo is very impressive... their courage and dedication are admirable."

On February 18, representatives associated with China resumed discussions with Jorasakuhuvo, and communication between the two sides returned to a normal course.

On March 11, an Airbus H130 helicopter from the Thai Ministry of Environment and Natural Resources circled three times over Jorasakuhuvo. Jorasakuhuvo welcomed the helicopter's presence.

Since then, Jorasakuhuvo has continued its development and gradually evolved into what it is today.
